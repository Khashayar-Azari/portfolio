Summary

Educational web application with a simple store

This educational application offers unique features tailored to enhance the user experience. Users have the ability to purchase and access online training courses directly through the platform. Additionally, users can opt to purchase specific segments of a course, providing flexibility and customization in their learning journey. During course playback, viewers' mobile numbers are randomly displayed on the video, facilitating easy access to downloadable content. Furthermore, users can minimize the video player for added convenience while navigating through the platform.

Moreover, the application boasts event management capabilities, allowing users to organize and host events seamlessly. Utilizing the educational calendar feature within the app, users can efficiently manage their time and schedule. Notably, the app incorporates a special subscription section, granting users access to all courses and educational materials. This subscription model enhances accessibility and affordability, enabling users to explore a wide range of content at their own pace.

In addition to its educational functionalities, the app includes a simple store feature, enabling users to sell physical products. This integrated store enhances the app's versatility, catering to both educational and commercial needs within a single platform. Overall, the app's diverse range of features, including online course access, event management, and e-commerce capabilities, make it a comprehensive solution for users seeking both educational and business opportunities.
